The Pigment Index

In collaboration with Kevin Smith

The pigment index was designed to be a resource for artists and researchers. It contains information on the various materials used to created artistâ€™s colors (for use in oil paint, pastels, dyes, watercolors, etc.).

The site is a filterable index (by color and by time of discovery) with info on each of the 23 included pigments with a general description, info about its source and, in some cases, manufacture.

Because we were unable to find aggregated data on the subject, the site has its own content management system for adding, updating, and removing information. The sites utility made itself even more apparent during the data entry process, as we had to reference a number of resources in order to get the info included in this initial build.
